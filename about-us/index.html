About Us

Welcome to Supreme Auto Body Collision Repair, your trusted destination for comprehensive car care. Conveniently located in the heart of Merrick Boulevard, NY, our shop is open every day to serve you better. We offer a full range of typical car repair and maintenance services, ensuring your vehicle runs smoothly and safely.

Our experienced technicians specialize in everything. Whether you need a quick tune-up or extensive repairs, we’ve got you covered. At Long Island City Auto Repair, we pride ourselves on our exceptional customer service, honest pricing, and commitment to quality. Drive in today and experience reliable, efficient, and friendly service you can count on.
